Brand Identity

LOGO CONCEPTS

Five directions for Agentic AI For Good. Pick your favourite — or tell Claude what to change.

Concept 01

Geometric Nodes

Three interconnected circular nodes forming an upward triangle with terracotta connecting lines. Suggests network, collaboration, and AI orchestration.

Warm beige bg Network motif Flat

Concept 02

Human + Signal

Abstract minimal human silhouette with a radiating arc/signal wave. Dark background edition — bold editorial feel. Signals human-AI collaboration.

Dark bg Human-AI Bold

Concept 03

Geometric Seedling

Abstract geometric seedling/sprout from simple rectangles and triangles. Suggests growth, positive impact, and building from foundations.

Warm beige bg Growth motif Minimal

Concept 04

Bold Letter Mark

Geometric "A" mark built from angular shapes with terracotta accent. Clean, scalable, and instantly recognisable as a monogram at any size.

Monogram Scalable Editorial

Concept 05

Orbit Rings

Concentric orbit rings with terracotta accents suggesting planetary motion or agent loops. Abstract, technical, and distinctly "agentic".

Abstract Agent loops Technical

Round 2 — Autumn Leaf Concepts

Leaf A

Geometric Flat Leaf

Clean maple/oak leaf silhouette built from flat polygons and triangles. Pure terracotta on beige — simple, scalable, instantly recognisable.

Geometric Flat Warm beige bg

Leaf B

Leaf + Network

Autumn leaf outline with AI network nodes at vein intersections. Bridges the organic/natural and the computational — warmth meets intelligence.

Network motif AI feel Warm beige bg

Leaf C

Deconstructed Leaf

Leaf broken into 3–4 offset geometric fragments suggesting motion, transformation, or emergence. Most abstract of the three — editorial edge.

Abstract Motion Edgy

Round 3 — Leaf A Iterations

Iteration 1

Ultra Minimal Angular

Sharpest version — pure angular polygons, no extras. Just the leaf mark and wordmark. Maximum simplicity, maximum scalability.

Ultra minimal Sharp Solid fill

Iteration 2

Outline Only

Leaf as a terracotta stroke with no fill — lighter, more refined. Works beautifully small. Elegant and editorial.

Line art No fill Refined

Iteration 3

Solid + Vein Line

Bold solid leaf with a single beige vein cut through the centre. Adds just enough detail to feel crafted without over-decorating.

Solid Detail vein Bold

Iteration 4

Double Leaf

Two overlapping geometric leaves in slightly different terracotta shades. Suggests depth, movement, and community — two things becoming one.

Layered Motion Community
