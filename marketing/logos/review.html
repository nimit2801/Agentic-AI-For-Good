Agentic AI For Good — Logo Concepts

Five directions on the existing brand palette (cream #F5F1EB, terracotta #D4754E, ink #1A1A1A). Each mark is shown on light and dark surfaces.

1. Spark Node

An agent as a connected node — three linked nodes with a spark at the center. Says "AI agents, taking action."

2. Arrow Wordmark

Typographic lockup with a chevron "A" that doubles as an arrow pointing forward. The most utilitarian option for headers and social.

3. Heart Circuit

A heart outlined in terracotta with circuit traces inside. Leans directly on "for good" — warmest of the set.

4. Sprout Agent

A small agent head growing out of a circuit board as two leaves. Ethical growth, open catalog, community-tended.

5. Hex Network

A hexagonal module with an "A" at its core, surrounded by a larger hex of six nodes. Reads as composable agents + catalog.
