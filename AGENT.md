# Agentic AI For Good

## What It Is
A discovery platform and knowledge base for AI tools, open-source projects, and agentic systems already in the market. We help builders, developers, founders, and students find what already exists — so they stop wasting hours prompting AI to research market tools and start building on top of what's already out there.

## The Problem We Solve
Every builder with an AI idea spends hours every day prompting ChatGPT/Claude to research what tools already exist, what's open-source, what competitors are doing. The results are often hallucinated, incomplete, or outdated. There is no single, curated, trustworthy source for "what's already out there in agentic AI."

## What We Do (Ordered by Priority)
1. **AI Tool Discovery & Knowledge Base** — A curated, searchable directory of AI tools, open-source projects, and agentic systems, categorized by use case, industry, and capability. This is the primary product.
2. **Offline Events** — IRL meetups, college workshops, and community events for professionals and students to discover tools, meet builders, and learn. Key differentiator in India — nobody else is doing this.
3. **GTM Support for AI Tools** — Help tools that need go-to-market support get discovered by the right audience through featured listings, sponsored stories, and event presence. This is how we make money.
4. **Editorial Stories** — Deep-dive case studies on how AI tools are being used in healthcare, education, climate, logistics, etc. Adds depth and soul, but NOT the highlight. Secondary focus.

## Who It's For
- **Builders/Developers** who want to know what tools exist before building from scratch
- **Founders** doing market research for their AI startup
- **Students** (IITs, NITs, tier-1 colleges) learning about the AI tool landscape
- **AI tool companies** that need distribution and GTM support in India
