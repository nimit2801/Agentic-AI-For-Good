# Agentic AI For Good

## What It Is
A discovery platform and knowledge base for AI tools, open-source projects, and agentic systems that are already in the market and doing amazing things. We help builders, developers, and founders find what already exists so they don't waste time reinventing or endlessly prompting AI to research market tools.

## The Problem We Solve
Every builder with an AI idea spends hours every day prompting ChatGPT/Claude to research what tools already exist, what's open-source, what competitors are doing. The results are often hallucinated, incomplete, or outdated. There is no single, curated, trustworthy source for "what's already out there in agentic AI."

## What We Do
1. **Curate AI tools & open-source projects** — A living knowledge base of what's in the market, categorized by use case, industry, and capability
2. **Write editorial stories** — Deep-dive case studies on how tools are being used in healthcare, education, climate, logistics, etc. (secondary focus, adds depth and soul)
3. **Host offline events** — IRL meetups and workshops for professionals and students to discover tools, meet builders, and learn
4. **GTM support for tools** — Help AI tools that need go-to-market support get discovered by the right audience through featured listings, sponsored stories, and event presence

## Who It's For
- **Builders/Developers** who want to know what tools exist before building from scratch
- **Founders** doing market research for their AI startup
- **Students** learning about the AI tool landscape
- **AI tool companies** that need distribution and GTM support
